BUY, RENT, FREEBIE

Q: What is Stevens Bazaar?

A: Stevens Bazaar is a shared economy platform for Stevens community. This website enables Stevens id holders to share everyday items with other Stevens id holders. You can sell it, rent it or just give away for free.


Q: Who can use Stevens Bazaar?

A: For now, Stevens Bazaar is just open to Stevens people i.e. anyone with Stevens email-id (@stevens.edu). But in the future, we are planning to take it to more colleges and finally open to everyone.


Q: How can I sign up?

A: First time users having a valid Stevens email-id (@stevens.edu) need to create an account on our website. Once signed up, just login and access it.


Q: How to get the item I want?

A: So far, we don't allow user to shop online. Buyers/Borrowers will look up products on our website and need to connect Seller via e-mail or telephone. Stevens Bazaar is not responsible for any trades which take place offline.


Q: How can I post an ad on Stevens Bazaar?

A: With a valid Stevens Bazaar account you can login and post an ad for selling, renting or giving for free. You need to put the information of this item, including name, type, description and price (not required for free stuff). Provide the way for people to contact you. Once posted, you ad will be up for only 15 days. You can extend this period by 15 more days before it expires. After 15 days, the ad will automatically get deleted and you need to repost it.
