Our website is based on the concept of Shared Economy, we believe that with Stevens Bazaar we can bring Stevens Community even closer.

Our idea comes from a very common complaint by Stevens' students: 'I want a new camera, but I can't afford the price right now.' After research, we found that instead of buying expensive items only for short term usage, people are willing to rent it from others. College students, have financial constraints and limited resources. We have tried to tap this willingness of people to rent items to create Stevens Bazaar. Where there is a need, there is a market.

The services we offering are similar to some of the popular websites such as Craigslist, Airbnb and Lyft.

But we offer three different services: Buy, Borrow and Freebie, and we only focus on college students (Stevens only right now).

At Stevens Bazaar, after sign up, user could post the item they want to sell, rent or give for free. The item could be anything, at any price, but will stay on our website for 15 days. The 15 days lifetime for ad posting is designed to keep the postings relevant.

Users can search for items then contact the owner to buy, borrow or take for free. So far we don't have communication system in Stevens Bazaar, every trade could be achieved offline.

This website is built for easy usage, everything is designed to achieve high quality user experience. In case you have questions or comments for Stevens Bazaar team, please feel free to contact us.
